Compilation of Job Responsibilities

Showcases Produced During My Work Period, Across Various Game Projects and Research Tasks. Produced by UITC Team in NetEase and Lilith Games.

Ego Effect

"Ego Effect" is a card game developed by NetEase, and it was the first game project I participated in since I started working. As a User Interface Technical Artist, my main responsibility was to recreate artistic designs within the game engine.

S6 Project of NetEase

"FallenMoon Plan"(unofficial name S6) is a first-person shooter game developed by NetEase for PC platform. My primary role in the project was to create effects for the interactive combat interface. The game boasted exceptionally high-quality artistic assets, providing me with valuable learning experiences. Unfortunately, it's regrettable that the project didn't successfully launch in the end.

LifeAfter

"LiftAfter" is a widely renowned game by NetEase. I collaborated with colleagues in User Experience Center to provide support for the interface effects in a new version of the game.

Art of Conouest 2

"Art of Conquest 2" was the first game project our team participated in after joining Lilith Games. During this project, we iterated on various in-game effects, especially designing and completing the production of the card-drawing animation. I organized numerous effect production into standardized effects tools during this period.
